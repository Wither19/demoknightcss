The Scripture Tag <scripture>

Used to cite from the Bible. Changes the contents inside to PT Serif and centers it (As a recommendation, use superscript for the numbers of the verses)

Use <verse> for dictating where the verse is found in the Bible, nested in <scripture>


Usage

 
<scripture><sup>8</sup>See to it that no one takes you captive through hollow
 and deceptive philosophy, which depends on human tradition and the elemental 
spiritual forces of this world rather than on Christ.
  <verse>Colossians 2:8 NIV</verse></scripture>

Result
